Profile

A research profile built around evidence production.

My academic work centers on political economy and development, especially conflict, media, crime, and institutions in Colombia. At the Institute for Replication, I organize replication games and AI Games projects, including papers involving more than 700 replicators and projects involving more than 2,000 replicators.

I also work across applied data science and behavioral economics, including demand forecasting, market analytics, human-AI research evaluation, and policy-facing diagnostics for private-sector and institutional settings.
