Research style

Applied questions, transparent workflows.

Across projects, the common thread is a concern for how social and political actors react to information. The empirical work usually connects high-frequency administrative or event data with quasi-experimental strategies, while the reproducibility work asks how research claims can be checked at scale.

Each project card gives readers the question, method family, and contribution before they open a paper.
